About Me

I'm Jackie, a software developer from Cleveland. I've spent many years in the finance and accounting field, and after working for two software companies, I desired to learn more about software development. I enrolled in We Can Code IT, a software engineering boot camp, and over the past several months, have learned C#, Object Oriented Programming, ASP.NET, MVC, SQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery through extensive hands-on instruction, from a simple console application to a database-first MVC application. I've found a new passion that lets me create exciting projects as well as a challenging yet fulfilling new career path.

I hope you'll take some time to enjoy my portfolio and see how my skills, talents, and professional background can help your company complete your next project and meet your goals.
